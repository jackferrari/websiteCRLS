Umass Amherst Overview

Umass Amherst was founded in 1863, and is the main school in the Umass system. In terms of computer science it is one of the top programs in the country. The computer science department is its own separate college in the university. Since Umass Amherst is a state school it is much larger than other schools like Northeastern. However the job placement rate after college is at a good 92%. The campus is located out in Amherst MA, about an hour and a half from boston. However it is not a completely cut off location. The town of Amherst is a very nice town. The campus itself is also fairly nice. With a few exceptions, the buildings are very nice looking. The campus also includes the tallest red brick library in North America, which is visible in a few of the pictures. Along with all of that Umass Amherst's dining options are rated very highly. While that is not the most important factor in college quality, it is a factor.

My Plans at Umass Amherst

At Umass Amherst I would likely go for a Computer Science BA. That way I would be able to continue doing classes on History alongside Computer Science. In terms of Computer Science departments, Umass Amherst is very strong. I know that I would be able to get a quality education there. For extra curricular activities, there is a Umass Amherst sailing team. I would most likely join up with that team so I could continue sailing in college. Umass Amherst also has a multitude of clubs that I would be able to join. Umass Amherst is my option of a big school. It has many of the educational merits of northeastern but far from a city center and on a much larger campus.
