University of Vermont Overview

The University of Vermont was founded in 1791, making it the oldest school on my list and among the oldest schools in the country. It was the fifth college founded in New England. The campus is located in Burlington VT. The UVM campus is a very beautiful campus and may be the prettiest school on the list. UVM is a state school and as a result, it has a fairly large student body. However despite being on the larger side, the schooldoes have a 93% job placement rate. The computer science program of UVM is also very highly regarded.
